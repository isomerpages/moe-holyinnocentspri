<h2><strong>Announcements</strong></h2>
<p></p>
<h4><strong>Racial Harmony Day Celebration [20 July 2026]</strong></h4>
<p>The school will commemorate Racial Harmony Day on <strong>Monday, 20 July 2026</strong>.
Anchored on this year’s theme, Our People, Our Tapestry, the commemoration
seeks to deepen students’ understanding of Singapore’s multicultural society
as a rich and interwoven fabric of diverse cultures, traditions, and shared
experiences. Through experiential learning and reflection, students will
develop an appreciation of harmonious relationships, empathy, and mutual
respect — values that strengthen social cohesion and contribute to a shared
Singaporean identity. As part of the commemoration, students are encouraged
to attend school dressed in traditional ethnic attire representing the
Malay, Indian, or Eurasian communities on the day.</p>
<p></p>
<h4><strong>National Day Celebration [7 August 2026]</strong></h4>
<p>In celebration of Singapore’s birthday, the school will celebrate National
Day on Friday, 7 August 2026. This commemoration seeks to deepen students’
appreciation of Singapore’s journey and the collective efforts that have
contributed to our nation-building. Through meaningful learning experiences
and reflection, we seek to develop in our students a stronger sense of
national identity, a deeper appreciation for unity in diversity, and the
student advocacy to be responsible and caring citizens in building a resilient,
inclusive, and cohesive Singapore.</p>
<p></p>
